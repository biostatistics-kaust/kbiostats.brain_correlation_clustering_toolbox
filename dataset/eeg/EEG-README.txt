EEG 

Data is collected from an epileptic patient at the 
Epilepsy Disorder Laboratory at the University of 
Michigan. Primary investigator is: Beth Malow, M.D.

Facts about the EEG dataset:

sampling rate 100Hz
scalp electrodes
they are differential electrodes (no reference)

of Bivariate Non-Stationary Time Series, Journal of the American Statistical 
Association,96, 543-560.

Improved Time-Frequency Analysis of Multi-channel EEG 
Using the SLEX Method, IEEE Transactions of Biomedical 
Engineering, 2002, accepted for publication. (Authors: Cranstoun, S., 
Ombao, H., von Sachs, R., Guo, W. and Litt, B.)

Guo, W., Dai, M., Ombao, H. and von Sachs (2003). Smoothing Spline ANOVA For 
Time-Dependent Spectral Analysis, Journal of the American Statistical

Stationary Time Series, Journal of the American Statistical Association, 100, 519-531.

Ombao, H. and Ho, M. (2006). Time-dependent frequency domain principal components

Ombao, H. and Van Bellegem (2008). Coherence Analysis: A Linear Filtering Point
